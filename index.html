Welcome to FONHARE!

The Haitian Foundation of Rehabilitation (FONHARE) is a grassroots Haitian non-profit organization dedicated to providing integrated physical and cognitive rehabilitation services to children and youth with disabilities in Haiti, especially those with a poor quality of life living with limited resources regardless their socioeconomic condition. FONHARE is the only organization providing rehab services in the northeast of Haiti to 13 cities with almost 500,000 citizens.
